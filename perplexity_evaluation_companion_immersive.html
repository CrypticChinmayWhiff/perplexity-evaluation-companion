What are global FinTech funding trends in Q3 2024?
Searched 847 sources • Generated 2.3 seconds
According to recent reports, global FinTech funding declined 23% year-over-year in Q3 2024, with total investments reaching $18.2 billion across 1,234 deals. This represents a continued slowdown from 2023's peak, though deal volume remained relatively stable compared to Q2 2024.

The decline is attributed to several factors, including macroeconomic uncertainty, rising interest rates, and increased regulatory scrutiny. Despite the overall decrease, certain sectors like embedded finance and B2B payments showed resilience with growth in their segments.
